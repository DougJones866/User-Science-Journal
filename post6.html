Project: Making things Accessable

Week 6 Assignment

Posted by Douglas Jones on May 12, 2019

Making things Accessable

Considering accessability early on can go a long way to ensuring your website will be able to used by just about without, ensuring everyone can get the same experience no matter their limitatation. While discussing accessability and evualating other websites it has made us begin to think about the ways we build our own websites as well.

Lables

Looking at my page I see that I have neglected to lable my pages. Would be helpful to know what each page is before clicking on them for those who are viewing the page besides me. It's better to have this taken care of early on rather than later once you have built up a huge list of different pages, then it can become very tedious.

After adding labels the pages are a lot more identifiable now. Fortunally I already made sure to give all my images alt tags, so that will save time not having to go through every image to add them in. It's generally important to make sure things are all labeled properly and to ensure that those lables are understandable. Right now this is a simple fix but for those who have been building their websites for years this can be quite the headache if not dealt with early on.

Tools

I ran my page through two tools Lighthouse and Wave

Looking at this it seems like my page got pretty good marks, but there are always ways to find improvements to increase performance and overall accessability of the website. The fact that I am using a bootstrap may have had a hand in it.

Improvements

There's a few things I would like to do in order to improve the accessability on my page. One would be to work in a way to add keyboard only navigation. Mainly in the way of switching from each page using the arrow keys for easy navigation.

I also think the page headers with the white text could have borders added to them to allow them to be more easily seen. Sometimes I think the white text may be difficult to see for some without the border.

Sumamry

Since i'm using a bootstrap for this page that might have been a reason why there were little problems that needed to be fixed from the audit. Bootstraps can come in handy to use in order to help minimize inaccessability issues, although i'm sure that's not the case with all of them. Accessability is highly important whether you use them or not. Just having the avaliable can go a long way to extenting the reach of your brand and into created a more inviting enviroment for every user.
