Project: Analyze for Usability

Week 2 Assignment

Posted by Douglas Jones on April 14, 2019

What site are you working with?

The site I am analyzing is Ifixit.com

What is the purpose of this site

This website was created to be a collection of information related to the repair of electronic devices. It contains detailed guides with instructions on how to do repairs.

What criteria are you using to analyze the site?

These are the criteria I will be using to analize the site.

  • Do I know where I am in this website?
  • Is the content easy to understand?
  • Is there a lot of noise?
  • What can be improved

Do I know where I am on this website?

I would say that it is easy to find you way around on this site. The pages aren't too cluttered and everything is seperated depending on device type and brand. There's also a search bar at the top of the screen if you already know what you're looking for.

Is the content easy to understand?

The instructions listed for a particular item is pretty easy to follow. Each step has a picture showing what you should be looking at and what you need to do. It also tells you the difficulty of a repair, how long it should take, and what tools are needed.

Is there a lot of noice?

When you're reading through the repair steps it's pretty straight and to the point as most steps are summed up in just one sentence. The rest of the site isn't cluttered either, as those who are coming to this website alreadu have an idea what they are looking for and have no need to be bogged down with other unrelated there.

What can be improved

The website and pretty straight foward and does what it sets out to do pretty well, but for some just following the images may not be enough. Some may prefer to have audio and video to follow to better understand a precedure.
